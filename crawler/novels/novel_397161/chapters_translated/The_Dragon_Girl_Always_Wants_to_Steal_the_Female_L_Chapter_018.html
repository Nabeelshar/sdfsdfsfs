Fish, surely! She herself was a carp spirit; was the director trying to send her back to her happy childhood? Pei Ning wasn't really interested in this ocean-themed restaurant. Finding seashells or not made no difference, and besides— She didn't want Qiao Song to be so tired. After listening to the director's explanation and glancing at the photos of the themed restaurant, Qiao Song turned to Pei Ning and asked, "Do you want to go?"
