Chapter 3 "Thank you, President Si and President Tian, for investing in our 'Reckless' production team. I'll drink this first as a sign of respect!" Si Sheng smiled insincerely, his eyes fixed on Wen Yanhe, who was quietly eating his meal. He simply couldn't believe that Li Yueyue hadn't arranged the seating. She had actually placed Wen Yanhe to Tang Xuyu's right. Throughout the meal, Si Sheng and Tian Xinyuan kept pouring drinks for Wen Yanhe. After a while, the crew members realized that Wen Yanhe had offended Si Sheng and Tian Xinyuan. The assistant was frantic. She quickly broke down trying to stop him. "Brother Wen really can't drink anymore! He really can't drink anymore! Please, President Si and President Tian, have mercy on Brother Wen!" The young woman was crying her eyes out. Qu Sisi knew that Tang Xuyu wanted Wen Yanhe to drink until he felt nauseous and never wanted to drink again. She wanted to stop him, but didn't want to offend the Tang family. She could only grab Li Yueyue and forcibly pull her away. Si Sheng and Tian Xinyuan looked at Tang Xuyu. "Even your brother Wen hasn't said anything. Do you, a mere assistant, have the right to speak before your master?" Tang Xuyu lazily raised his eyelids, his eyes filled with a ruthless intensity that made the young girl's legs go weak, and tears welled up in her eyes. Wen Yanhe's body stiffened for a moment. Si Sheng and Tian Xinyuan both obeyed Tang Xuyu, indicating that Tang Xuyu held the dominant position among the three. The fact that Tang Xuyu didn't stop them from pouring him so much wine suggests that he tacitly approved of their actions. "I didn't say stop, so keep drinking." Si Sheng smiled and refilled Wen Yanhe's glass. "Drinking like this is pointless." Tian Xinyuan, in full view of everyone, placed a poisoned wine into Wen Yanhe's glass. Wen Yanhe's face was deathly pale, his lips devoid of color. His hand holding the glass trembled slightly. "Drink! Poison!" Wen Yanhe swallowed the poisoned wine without leaving a drop. Si Sheng and Tian Xinyuan smiled sarcastically. He turned and, without missing a single person from the gathering, made an excuse to send them all back to their hotel. Even Wen Yanhe's assistant was escorted away by bodyguards. "Ayu, we'll be leaving now." "Okay." Tang Xuyu finished eating, wiped his mouth, stood up, and walked to Wen Yanhe's right side. "Wen Yanhe, [someone's name], [something unclear]. Put away your shameful thoughts." Wen Yanhe silently shed tears. "Where are you staying? I'll take you home." "Thank you." Tang Xuyu opened the back door of the car for Wen Yanhe to get in, then stepped inside himself. Tang Xuyu turned his head to look at the scenery outside the window, his thoughts drifting into the distance. Wen Yanhe couldn't forcefully suppress the surging effects of the drug. He bit his lip tightly, tears streaming silently down his face. "Look up." Wen Yanhe was lost in his own world, oblivious to the outside world. "Wen Yanhe?" He hadn't expected Wen Yanhe to become like this. Tang Xuyu grabbed Wen Yanhe's chin and skillfully forced his lips open, stuffing the medicine into Wen Yanhe's mouth. Tang Xuyu wanted Wen Yanhe to swallow water to take the medicine down his throat. Tang Xuyu watched the water slide down his chin. He knew this couldn't continue. He poured water into Wen Yanhe's mouth, gripped his chin tightly, and pressed their lips together. Wen Yanhe unconsciously began to swallow the water. Fortunately, the medicine was finally swallowed. The next day, Wen Yanhe woke up, looking blankly at the room's furnishings. Luxurious yet simple. Something the Wen family could never afford in their lifetime. Class was something they couldn't change, no matter how hard they climbed. The moment Wen Yanhe saw Tang Xuyu, he recalled what had happened in the car the previous night. Wen Yanhe buried his feelings for Tang Xuyu deep in his heart. Li Yueyue learned from Qu Sisi that Tang Xuyu's identity was far from simple. Although the Si family was an upper-class family, there was an insurmountable gap between them and the Tang family. Li Yueyue had to admit that the Tang family was a family they could only look up to.
