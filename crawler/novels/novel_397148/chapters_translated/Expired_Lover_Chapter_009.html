Whenever Grandpa Liang hadn't chosen a style, he would subconsciously wear it. “Should we change it?” Xia Sang asked. “No need.” After saying that, Liang Yiming dressed, went downstairs, and took his car to work. At that time, he was visiting the set of the newly invested S drama. After watching for a while, he got bored and went for a walk. He unknowingly wandered into a small theater, where a boy wearing a long-sleeved shirt was hiding in the shadows of a tent. The boy turned his head back and forth several times after seeing him, his face flushed, whether from the heat or shyness, it was hard to tell. After a while, the boy seemed to have made up his mind and ran out, bringing him a bottle of water and a small fan. “Brother, which film crew are you with?” “Next door.” “Next door…it seems to be the ‘I’m a Charmer’ theater…” the boy muttered softly, “Are you a lead actor?” “No.” Liang Yiming, holding the water in his hand, was in a good mood and patiently answered the other's question. “A supporting role.” Liang Yiming shook his head. "So you're just another cannon fodder extra like me!" The boy suddenly seemed to have found his group, beaming with joy. Realizing he'd been too excited, he became a little embarrassed. "I didn't expect you to be so handsome! And just an extra." The director's going to yell at you!" "Oh, oh..." The boy then fumbled around, seemingly without his phone. The man on the other end kept urging, "Xia Sang! What are you doing?!" "We...we'll see you next time! My name is Xia Sang, next time! See you next time!" He looked at him disappointedly and hurriedly left. Before Liang Yiming could respond, the boy quickly left, returning to the scene. That was the first time Liang Yiming met Xia Sang. Passionate, youthful. For a fleeting moment, an itchy sensation traveled from her gaze to his body. Perhaps it was because of that instant that he gave Xia Sang the opportunity to get close to him when they met again, and later it proved that Xia Sang was a good lover.
