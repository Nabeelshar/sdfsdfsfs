Chapter 1 01 Reunion with an Old Flame Xia Sang followed behind her manager, Brother Jin, secretly rubbing her stomach. She was too full from the meal she'd just eaten. "What's wrong? Why are you walking so slowly today?" Jin Fulai turned around, looking at his artist with disdain. "I'm too full." Xia Sang glanced at the hotel entrance, moved closer, and whispered. Jin Fulai looked helpless. "I'm telling you, when you were with Master Liang, what kind of delicious food didn't you eat? What kind of delicacies didn't you order? Be more mature!" "But I've terminated my contract with Master Liang now." Xia Sang's mouth watered at the thought of all the delicious meals she'd had before. "Now, one meal might mean one less." This was the most luxurious hotel in B City, where land is incredibly expensive! "He'll never have a chance to come again." "Pah, pah, pah! Say something auspicious! Can't we be a little more successful?!" Jin Fulai was also extremely worried. He recalled signing Xia Sang back then because of Xia Sang's handsome face and seemingly clever nature. He thought Xia Sang might be a promising talent, someone he could take care of in the future. He never expected that this guy was just a pretty face. But then Jin Fulai thought about it again. He himself had only been doing well these past two years thanks to Xia Sang. "When we become famous, we'll have plenty of opportunities to eat." "Then I'd better try to eat as much as possible now." Xia Sang was very self-aware. "...Don't you believe you'll become famous?" "Do you believe it?" Xia Sang retorted to Brother Jin. “I used to believe, but now I only have a little,” Jin Fulai sighed. “Why can’t you ever get ahead?” “Don’t people like me?” “Everyone says it’s a looks-obsessed world now. Looking like you, how come you’re doing so poorly?” “Maybe it’s because you’re uneducated?” “You’ve been with Master Liang for two years, and you’re still a nobody.” “Maybe it’s because you’re unskilled?” “Go away, go away, that’s like stabbing someone in the heart.” Xia Sang smiled, this time saying nothing. “Alright, alright, you’re only 21, with a bright future ahead of you. Let’s go home.” Jin Fulai waved his hand, gesturing for Xia Sang to follow him. But before they’d taken a few steps, Xia Sang suddenly pulled Jin Fulai away. He turned around, about to ask what was wrong, when he heard Xia Sang speak. "Hey! Hey! Brother Jin, Father Liang's here!" Xia Sang exclaimed excitedly. Several people entered the hotel. The man at the head of the group was over 1.9 meters tall, dressed in a black suit. He was tall and straight, with sharp eyebrows, slightly dark skin, and chiseled features. He was a man of few words and reserved demeanor, making it somewhat difficult to look directly at him. Beside him was a handsome young man, following closely behind. Three other men in suits lagged slightly behind, presumably assistants or bodyguards. Upon seeing the other person, Jin Fulai immediately pulled Xia Sang behind a pillar, shouting at the top of his lungs, "Shut up! Shut up!" Jin Fulai wiped away a cold sweat. He didn't know why Master Liang was here today. Although Master Liang was their big boss, he wouldn't dare rush up to greet him even if he had ten lives. After all, two months ago, Master Liang had ended Xia Sang's sugar daddy relationship and signed a new contract. Now that the new girl was smiling, rushing up now would be like picking up manure in a lantern—asking for trouble. After hiding with lingering fear, Jin Fulai turned around and saw Xia Sang peeking out to watch the commotion. His blood pressure was about to rise. This wretched thing! Xia Sang was the first artist he mentored during his mid-career transition, but unfortunately, he remained relatively unknown for two years, constantly languishing on the sidelines. Back then, he had spotted Xia Sang among the many extras in Hengdian World Studios. He had initially intended to sign him, but before the company could approve, Xia Sang found a major investor and ended up working for him. This shocked Jin Fulai. Upon learning the situation, he felt powerless and angry, wanting to put Xia Sang back in his mother's womb. But the contract was already signed. Jin Fulai lacked the ability and courage to tear it up with his bare hands at Liang Ye's place. He could only keep a close eye on Xia Sang's movements during this time, preventing him from doing anything foolish. When the contract expired, Liang Ye didn't renew it. Jin Fulai immediately brainwashed Xia Sang, insisting that the contract shouldn't be renewed. Even with countless suitors in the entertainment industry, being lovers isn't something to be proud of. After Liang Yiming and his group went inside, Jin Fulai and Xia Sang emerged from behind a pillar, quickly got into their car, and drove away. After driving about a mile, Jin Ge suddenly realized something. "Hey? Why did you call Grandpa Liang 'Dad' just now?" Jin Ge's heart skipped a beat. "Does Grandpa Liang have a habit of being called 'Dad'?" "No, Grandpa Liang likes seeing people begging and pleading." Xia Sang took a jar of candy from the back seat. He was naturally thin, so Jin Fulai didn't restrict his food intake much, but he was worried about his teeth getting cavities. "Every time I call him 'Dad' in bed, he finishes quickly, otherwise he'd keep me up all night. Why doesn't he let me call him 'Grandpa'?" "You've called Grandpa Liang 'Grandpa' before?" Jin Fulai asked curiously. "Yes, I have." "What was Grandpa Liang's reaction?" "He went limp, and even beat me up." "Haha..." Xia Sang, you idiot. Xia Sang didn't tell Jin Fulai what happened next. After he shouted, before Liang Ye even lost his temper, he couldn't help but burst out laughing. Finally, Liang Yiming coldly tossed Xia Sang out. He had originally planned to find the nearest hotel to sleep the night, but he forgot that they had gone to a villa halfway up the mountain. Xia Sang stared blankly at the endless mountain road and the dark mountain scenery in the distance. Would the taxi driver ever make it up? The cold wind halfway up the mountain almost made Xia Sang lose his mind. Finally, he saw a miniature version of Radish's dog villa, and shamelessly went to rub against Radish's large doghouse. That night, he found Radish's doghouse quite warm and comfortable, though it was a bit cramped between them.
