Chapter 49 29 Press Conference (Supplement) The next day, Xia Sang lay on her bed in her apartment, watching the live stream of the press conference on her phone. Mr. Liang was still wearing his usual black suit, the impeccable color concealing his fingers, gloved in black leather, pointing firmly and decisively at the display screen behind him. This was a clarification of the previously circulated information about the new drug. The incident of a mother and daughter dying simultaneously due to the new drug from the Liang Group was caused by improper medication administration by the patient's family. During the normal treatment process, the patient's family privately administered self-purchased tonics to the patient. The tonics produced a drug reaction that interacted with the new drug. Fortunately, the interaction between the previous medication and the tonics was not as strong, so it wasn't discovered in time. After switching to a new medication, significant drug interactions led to an excessive burden on the body, which the already weakened patient could not withstand, indirectly causing death. "These are the relevant medical test results, and we are deeply saddened by these findings," Liang Yiming explained simply, then listed the test reports. The flashing lights illuminated Liang Yiming's face, his deep features and resolute gaze making him even more charismatic. The flashes continued, and Liang Yiming controlled the pace of the entire press conference. "At the same time, we are also verifying and initiating investigations with relevant departments. Those who spread rumors about our company using unverified information will be prosecuted according to law for the social impact and reputational damage caused!" Liang Yiming's voice grew increasingly firm and powerful. “This new drug is designed to make treatment costs and effectiveness more affordable for patients. We hope everyone can learn more about the new treatment jointly developed by Liang's and Ye's Pharmaceuticals. Thank you!” After saying this, Liang Yiming stood up and bowed in thanks. The spotlight shone on him, making him radiant. The press conference lasted about one to two hours. Xia Sang, staring at his phone for so long, felt his eyes ache and become dry. He covered his phone with his hand, looked at the ceiling, and comforted himself, “I'm just sad for a little while; I'll be fine soon.” * After this press conference, netizens' opinions and sentiments immediately changed.
